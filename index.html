RAINBOW COSMETICS

Rainbow Cosmetics LLC is a cosmetics brand by Ruziya, that was launched on June 18, 2008. The brand began with launching it's skin care line, Rainbow Skin which instantly blew up. The original skin care set launch included 25 skin scare kits, and has since expanded to 50. Ruziya then launched 5 other branches which include, Rainbow Lip Kit, Rainbow Scents, and Rainbow Hair, in that particular order. The inclusion of Rainbow Skin includes skin care for 50 different skin types, offering a wide variety for all skin types.
